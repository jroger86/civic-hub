      A structured model for emergency guidance. Build with experienced responders, public health, and community organizers.

      About

      Civic Hub is a dual-purpose civic platform: everyday participation + critical infrastructure guidance.

      • Issues: report, route, and track
      • Live Debate: keep the community’s “town hall” alive
      • Petitions: convert discussion into outcomes
      • Emergency Guidance: Crisis Mode shifts UI priority during disasters
