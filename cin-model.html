Community Information Network (CIN)

This is the “community nervous system” idea: a predictable loop for how information is submitted, triaged, verified, and surfaced without burning out volunteers.

Flow

  1. Citizens submit signals: issues, petitions, crisis reports.
  2. Signals get triaged: urgency, safety, feasibility.
  3. Outputs: public dashboards, petitions, coordinated response.

Promises

  • Clarity over noise
  • Safety-first routing in crisis
  • Public accountability through transparent signals
