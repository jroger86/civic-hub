About Civic Hub

Civic Hub is a prototype “community signal + action” platform built to keep residents informed and activated—from everyday community updates to crisis guidance.

Roadmap

  • Persistent signup + authentication (real accounts)
  • Issue triage with transparent prioritization
  • Petition validation + signature verification
  • Accessibility pass (keyboard-first nav, ARIA, high contrast)
